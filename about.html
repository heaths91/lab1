About

Web design is very new to me, but I think I am getting the hang of it.
In my spare time I play on a wheelchair basketball team the Tacoma Titans.
Along with GIS and geography, the outdoors are very important to me, but it is also this love for the outdoors that led me to living in a chair.

While mountain biking in 2015, here in the Teanaway region of eastern Washington, I crashed, leaving me with a spinal cord injury and paralyzed from the chest down. Although that may sound very grim, my life following has been full of amazing experiences. I am truly blessed and have a fantastic life, with an amazing group of people around me including my girlfriend and fantastic family. Thank you for hearing a little bit more about me!
